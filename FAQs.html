Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the Retdem-Ber-Me system.

1. What is this website for?

This website is designed to help students track their return demonstration (retdem) requirements and borrow necessary materials in advance. It serves as both a checklist tracker and a material reservation system.

2. Who can use this website?

The website is intended for nursing students who are required to complete return demonstrations and need access to related materials.

3. How do I check my retdem checklist?

You can log in to your account and view your assigned checklist. It will show completed and pending tasks for your return demonstrations.

4. How will I know if my retdem is completed or approved?

Your checklist will be updated by your instructor, and the status will reflect whether your return demonstration is completed or still pending.

5. How do I borrow materials?

Go to the “Borrowing” section, select the materials you need, choose your preferred date, and submit a request. Wait for confirmation before claiming the items.

6. Is there a limit to how many materials I can borrow?

Yes, there may be a limit depending on availability and school guidelines to ensure fair access for all students.

7. Can I reserve materials in advance?

Yes, the system allows you to reserve materials ahead of your scheduled retdem to ensure availability.

8. What happens if I return materials late?

Late returns may result in penalties or temporary restriction from borrowing again, depending on the rules set by your department.

9. What should I do if the materials I need are unavailable?

You can check back later for availability or coordinate with your instructor or classmates for alternatives.

10. Who do I contact if I encounter problems with the website?

You can contact the system administrator or your assigned faculty for assistance with login issues, checklist concerns, or borrowing problems.
